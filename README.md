# Titan
### Internal Label Management System  
**Proprietary Software – Glass House Studios**

---

## Overview

**Titan** is a proprietary internal software platform developed exclusively for **Glass House Studios** to assist label executives with artist management, release planning, analytics tracking, and operational workflows.

The system is designed to centralize information related to:

- Artist profiles
- Release schedules
- Marketing campaigns
- Performance analytics
- Internal notes and label communications

Titan is **not public software** and is intended strictly for **authorized personnel of Glass House Studios and its affiliated entities**.

---

## Intended Use

Titan is developed solely for **internal operational use by Glass House Studio label executives and authorized staff**.

The platform supports:

- Artist relationship management
- Music release coordination
- Marketing and campaign planning
- Data organization
- Label workflow automation

Titan is **not intended for external distribution, resale, or third-party access**.
